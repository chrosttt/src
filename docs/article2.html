The Future of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries, from healthcare to transportation. Its rapid evolution raises questions about ethics, jobs, and human-AI collaboration.

Applications of AI

  • Autonomous vehicles
  • Medical diagnostics
  • Natural language processing
  • Financial predictions

Ethical Considerations

AI introduces challenges such as bias, privacy concerns, and job displacement. Policies and responsible AI design are essential to ensure technology benefits society.
